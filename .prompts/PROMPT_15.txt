      go:         `main.go.template`, `go.mod.template`
      dotnet:     `Program.cs.template`, `*.csproj.template`
    `README.template.md`

  Stack secrets are referenced via `pulumi config get --secret`, never
  inlined. Resource names include the stack name as suffix.
